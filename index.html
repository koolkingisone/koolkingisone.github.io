About

An engineer who takes ideas from CAD to validated hardware.

MK

My work sits where mechanical design, structural analysis and engineering automation meet. At Heavy Industries Taxila I ran finite-element analysis on armoured vehicle hulls and produced the mechanical design and drawings for a Remote Control Weapon Station; as a cost estimator I built digital-portal and automation workflows that made estimation faster and more reliable.

I'm now pursuing an M.Sc. in Aeronautical Engineering at Linköping University, extending that mechanical foundation toward lightweight aerospace structures, flight mechanics and simulation-driven design. I care about work that is rigorous, well-documented and genuinely manufacturable — and I learn fastest when a team is solving a hard problem together.
